AGENTS

Duelist is the offense class in VALORANT, with agents focusing on 1vs1 combat and with a mission to get highest death-count for their team. Their abilities focus on inflicting damage and getting the most out of their kills. However, every agent is different from each other and with their own special abilities. In the Duelist class, you will find agents Jett, Phoenix, Reyna, Raze, Yoru, Neon and Iso.

SentinelsThe Sentinels agents in VALORANT have a defensive role in the team composition. Their abilities focus on slowing down or stopping the enemies altogether by utilizing different techniques, such as walls and more specific items unique to each agent. In the Sentinel class, you will find agents Deadlock, Sage, Killjoy, Cypher, and Chamber.

ControllersIf you are into strategic play, Controllers might be your bread and butter. These agents shape the combat terrain with their smokes and walls, impeding enemy’s vision and pushing them into choke points. Whether you're attacking or defending, Controllers and their smokes are invaluable to the team. In the Controller class, you will find agents Harbor, Astra, Brimstone, Viper, and Omen.

Initiators are the recon class in VALORANT; these agents’ main duty is opening the road for Duelist and other teammates. They have powerful tools to scan the area, like Sova’s arrows, or flash grenades, to secure a safe passage into the chosen map’s areas. Agents Sova, Breach, Skye, KAY/O, Fade, and Gekko are all part of the Initiators class.
